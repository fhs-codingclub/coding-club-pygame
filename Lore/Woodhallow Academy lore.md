Woodhallow Academy  
Lore/time line  
\~Time line\~  
SPOILERS\!

1330- A group of scientists called the "Academics" head below ground to start a civilization of their own

1332- The Academics find an alter deep under ground beneath it they find a race dying race of men who are part dragon, who call themselves the Dragon Born

1333- The Academics and Dragon Born continue farther into the earth

1334- The Academics and Dragon Born reach the deepest they will go, and find another altar which contains an old and dark ancient evil which they unintentionally release and cause the black plague and gain dark magic powers

1335- The Academics blame the Dragon Born for releasing the Ancient one and begin enslaving them.

1339- The first Dragon Born is killed for disobedience  
1345- The last free Dragon Born is enslaved

1356- The Ancient one is contained and the Black plague ends 

1900- A small group of Academics head to the surface and have kids with the surface dwellers

1910- The first child of a Academic and a surface dweller returns to the Under Ground and his dark magic is activated

1920- Dragon Born start protesting their enslavement 

1921- A civil war breaks out underground

1930- The underground civil war ends

1940- The Skuld family becomes the most wealthy Dragon Born family

1999- A powerful Dragon Born civil rights activist gives his first speech

2009- Kara Skuld is born

20011- Lumi Skuld is born  
2013- The Skuld family are the victims of the last anti-Dragon Born attack, Kara Skuld is the only survivor… or is she? 

2014- Kara is adopted by Dr. Pauling, Kara grows up hating humanity

2019- Kara starts living at Woodhallow Academy



2025- The story begins
